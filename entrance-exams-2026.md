### 8. CLAT 2027 (Common Law Admission Test)
**Conducted by:** Consortium of National Law Universities (NLUs)  
**For:** BA LLB (5-year) and LLM admissions in all 24 National Law Universities — NLU Delhi, NLSIU Bangalore, NALSAR Hyderabad, and others  
**Qualification:** 12th pass (for UG) / LLB (for PG)  

| Date | Detail |
|---|---|
| Registration opens | **August 1, 2026** |
| Last date to apply | **October 31, 2026** |
| Exam date | **December 6, 2026** |

**Official website:** consortiumofnlus.ac.in  
**Exam mode:** Offline (pen and paper)  
**Paper:** 120 MCQ questions, 2 hours, -0.25 negative marking  

CLAT is the single entrance test for all the NLUs, which are the top law colleges in India. The exam tests English comprehension, legal reasoning, logical reasoning, quantitative skills, and current affairs. For anyone serious about a law career, an NLU degree is the best starting point.
