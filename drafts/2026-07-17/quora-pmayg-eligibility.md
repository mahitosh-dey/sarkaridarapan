## Draft

PMAY-Gramin eligibility works on two lists now, not one, and most Quora answers still describe only the older SECC 2011 list.

**List 1 — SECC 2011 (original beneficiary pool).** The Ministry of Rural Development picked eligible households from the Socio-Economic and Caste Census 2011, applying the housing-deprivation parameters (kutcha wall + kutcha roof, or no roof). That gave the original beneficiary universe of about 2.95 crore households. This list is nearly saturated. As of 30 June 2026, 3.90 crore houses have been sanctioned and 2.99 crore completed per the PMAY-G dashboard on pmayg.nic.in.

**List 2 — Awaas Plus 2024 (fresh survey).** The Union Cabinet approved 2 crore additional homes under PMAY-G on 9 August 2024, plus a fresh survey called Awaas+ 2024 to identify households missed by the 2011 SECC. The survey ran from October 2024 to March 2025 via a mobile app. If you were left out of the original SECC list, this is the list you need to be on. Panchayats are still finalising Awaas+ 2024 rankings as of July 2026.

**Exclusion criteria (both lists):** You're not eligible if your household has any of these:

- Motorised two/three/four wheeler
- Mechanised three/four wheel agricultural equipment
- Kisan Credit Card with limit above Rs. 50,000
- Any member earning above Rs. 15,000/month (revised from Rs. 10,000 in the Awaas+ 2024 guidelines)
- Any member paying income tax or professional tax
- Refrigerator, landline phone, or 2.5 acres+ of irrigated land or 5 acres+ of unirrigated land
- A pucca house of two or more rooms already

**Subsidy amount:** Rs. 1.20 lakh in plain areas and Rs. 1.30 lakh in hilly/difficult/IAP areas, released in three or four instalments tied to construction progress. On top of that:

- MGNREGA labour wages of 90-95 days (roughly Rs. 20,000-25,000 depending on state MGNREGA rate)
- Rs. 12,000 SBM-G subsidy for a toilet
- Free LPG connection under Ujjwala if eligible
- Free electricity connection under Saubhagya if eligible

Total effective benefit works out to roughly Rs. 1.70 lakh for a plain-area beneficiary, Rs. 1.90-2.00 lakh for a hilly-area one.

**How to check if you're on the list:** Go to pmayg.nic.in, click "Stakeholders", then "IAY / PMAYG beneficiary", enter your registration number. If you don't have one, ask your gram panchayat sachiv to confirm your Awaas+ 2024 survey status.

I've done a full walk-through with the 2026 sanction/completion state-wise, three-instalment payment timing, and the appeal process if your name is missing: [PMAY-Gramin 2026 guide](https://www.sarkaridarapan.com/sarkari-yojana/pm-awas-yojana-gramin-pmay-g-2026).
