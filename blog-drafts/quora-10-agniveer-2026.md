## Answer draft (for "How do I join Agniveer?")

To join Agniveer in 2026, you have three parallel application paths: Army Agniveer CEE, Navy SSR / MR, and IAF Agniveer Vayu. Each has separate notifications, separate exams, and separate portals. You can apply to more than one in the same year (recruitment cycles overlap only partially). Here is exactly what each path requires and how to pick the right one.

**Step 1: Check basic eligibility across all three entries.**

- Age: 17.5 to 21 years at the time of enrolment (for most 2026 batches).
- Marital status: unmarried. You cannot marry during the 4-year service period.
- Nationality: Indian citizen (Nepal candidates eligible under separate provisions).
- Education: 10th pass minimum for Army General Duty; 10+2 for Army Technical, all Navy SSR / MR, and all IAF Agniveer Vayu trades.
- Physical standards: minimum height 152.5 cm for IAF (relaxations for North East, Ladakh, Sikkim, Andaman). Army and Navy have region-specific standards.

**Step 2: Pick the entry that fits your profile.**

Agniveer Vayu (Air Force): about 2,500 vacancies per batch, 1 in 250 selection ratio, PFT run of 1.6 km under 6:30, and the strictest English cut-off (50% in English separately, not just aggregate). Portal: agnipathvayu.cdac.in. Best fit for arts and non-technical 10+2 candidates because the paper has Reasoning plus General Awareness alongside English.

Agniveer Army CEE: about 40,000 vacancies per batch, 1 in 300 selection ratio for General Duty, toughest PFT (1.6 km under 5:30), and the simplest written test. Portal: joinindianarmy.nic.in. Best fit for candidates with a strong physical fitness baseline who prefer a shorter written paper.

Navy SSR (Senior Secondary Recruit) and MR (Matric Recruit): about 2,500 vacancies per batch, 1 in 400 selection ratio, easier PFT (1.6 km under 7:00), and the toughest written test (100 questions in 60 minutes covering Science, Math, English, and General Knowledge). Portal: joinindiannavy.gov.in. Best fit for Science stream 10+2 students with speed-plus-accuracy math ability.

**Step 3: Register on the correct portal and complete the online application.**

Each entry has its own portal. You will need a valid email ID, mobile number, recent passport-size photo (JPEG, white background), signature, thumb impression, and 10th or 12th mark sheet. Fees are Rs. 250 for IAF Agniveer Vayu, Rs. 250 for Navy SSR, and typically nil for Army CEE (nominal Rs. 250 in some batches). Download the confirmation and admit card as instructed.

**Step 4: Understand what you are signing up for.**

The 4-year contract is real. 75% of Agniveers exit after 4 years with a Rs. 10.04 lakh Seva Nidhi payout (fully tax-free), a service record, and a skill certificate. Only 25% are retained as regular Army, Navy, or IAF personnel through a performance evaluation in the fourth year. If you join expecting the old 20-year Sepoy or Airman contract, you are joining under the wrong assumption.

What Agniveer service gives you beyond the Rs. 10 lakh corpus:

1. **Seva Nidhi corpus of Rs. 10.04 lakh** at exit. Your contribution is Rs. 5.02 lakh across 4 years (30% of monthly package); government matches it plus interest, and the entire amount is tax-free.
2. **10% reservation in CAPF recruitment** (CRPF, BSF, CISF, ITBP, SSB) for constable and ASI posts. UP, Haryana, MP, and Uttarakhand have announced 10 to 20% quotas in state police recruitment for exited Agniveers.
3. **Age relaxation of 3 to 5 years** in future government job applications. If you exit at 25, you effectively get access to central government jobs typically restricted to under-27 candidates.
4. **Skill certificate recognised across PSUs** for lateral entry. Useful for railway apprentice, port authority, and infrastructure roles.
5. **Military discipline and network** that opens doors most 22-year-old graduates cannot access.

**Step 5: Prepare for the written test and PFT in parallel.**

The written test is a 45 to 60 minute speed paper. Start with NCERT Class 11 to 12 for Physics and Maths (if IAF), R.S. Aggarwal for Reasoning, and Lucent GK for General Awareness. Solve 3 previous year papers per week in the last month before the exam. For PFT, start the 1.6 km run on Day 1 of preparation because building from a sedentary base to under 6:30 takes 6 to 10 weeks minimum.

**One thing candidates miss:** the Rs. 30,000 monthly package sounds low, but the in-hand of Rs. 21,000 (after the 30% Seva Nidhi contribution) plus free accommodation, free medical, and dress allowance means your effective take-home in a metro city is comparable to a private sector Rs. 35,000 job with expenses accounted for.
