# Agniveer Army CEE Result 2026: how to check at joinindianarmy.nic.in

*Original guide first published on [sarkaridarapan.com](https://www.sarkaridarapan.com/blog/agniveer-army-cee-result-2026-how-to-check). I run sarkaridarapan, an ad-free tracker for Indian government jobs and exam updates. This is the same guide, mirrored here for readers who prefer Medium.*

The Agniveer Army CEE (Common Entrance Examination) Result 2026 is published on joinindianarmy.nic.in. Candidates who appeared for the written exam after clearing the Physical Fitness Test and Medical Examination can check their qualifying status using their roll number and date of birth.

This guide covers how to check the result, what the result slip shows, and what happens next for selected candidates.

## How to check Agniveer Army CEE Result 2026

1. Open the official website: joinindianarmy.nic.in
2. Look for the "Agniveer CEE Result 2026" link on the homepage or under the Recruitment section.
3. Click the result link for your recruitment batch.
4. Enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth (DD/MM/YYYY format).
5. Submit and view your result.
6. Download and save the result slip as a PDF.

Your result slip will show your roll number, name, category (GD, Technical, Clerk/SKT, or Tradesman), marks obtained in the CEE, and qualifying status. Selected candidates will also see reporting instructions on the slip.

## Recruitment categories

Agniveer Army recruitment covers five categories. Your result applies to the category you registered for.

- **Soldier General Duty (GD)**: Class 10 with 45% aggregate. Exam duration 60 minutes.
- **Soldier Technical**: Class 12 with PCM and English, 50% aggregate. Exam duration 60 minutes.
- **Soldier Clerk / Store Keeper Technical (SKT)**: Class 12 (any stream), 60% aggregate, with at least 50% in English and Math. Exam duration 60 minutes.
- **Soldier Tradesman (Class 10)**: Class 10 with 33% in each subject. Exam duration 60 minutes.
- **Soldier Tradesman (Class 8)**: Class 8 pass for specific trades: Cook, House Keeper, Mess Keeper, Gardener, Safaiwala. Exam duration 60 minutes.

The selection process puts physical fitness and medical qualification before the written exam. Candidates who cleared the Physical Fitness Test (PFT) and Medical Examination at the rally ground are eligible to sit the CEE. The merit list after CEE is based on your written exam performance within your category.

## What happens after the result

### Document verification

Selected candidates receive reporting orders specifying the date and location for document verification. You must bring original certificates for:

- Class 10 and Class 12 marksheets and certificates
- School leaving certificate or Transfer Certificate (TC)
- Caste certificate (OBC, SC, ST), if applicable
- Domicile certificate
- Character certificate from a gazetted officer or village sarpanch
- Recent passport-size photographs
- Aadhaar card

Do not bring photocopies only. Original documents are checked physically. Missing or mismatched documents result in disqualification at this stage.

### Training

Soldiers who complete document verification and final enrollment are sent to the respective training centre for their arm or service. Agniveer GD candidates generally go to the Regimental Training Centres of their respective Infantry regiments. Training typically lasts 24 to 31 weeks.

### Service and Seva Nidhi

Agniveers serve for 4 years. Monthly package increases each year, per the Ministry of Defence notification of June 2022:

- **Year 1**: Rs. 30,000 monthly package, Rs. 21,000 in-hand pay, Rs. 9,000 contribution to Seva Nidhi.
- **Year 2**: Rs. 33,000 monthly package, Rs. 23,100 in-hand pay, Rs. 9,900 contribution to Seva Nidhi.
- **Year 3**: Rs. 36,500 monthly package, Rs. 25,580 in-hand pay, Rs. 10,950 contribution to Seva Nidhi.
- **Year 4**: Rs. 40,000 monthly package, Rs. 28,000 in-hand pay, Rs. 12,000 contribution to Seva Nidhi.

The government matches the Agniveer's contribution to the Seva Nidhi fund. After 4 years, the total corpus is approximately Rs. 11.71 lakh, paid out tax-free, per the official scheme notification.

25% of Agniveers from each batch are offered the option to continue in regular Army service after 4 years. The selection for this 25% is based on performance during service.

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I check my Agniveer Army CEE Result 2026?**
Go to joinindianarmy.nic.in, click on the result link for your recruitment batch, enter your Roll Number and Date of Birth, and download your result slip.

**What documents do I need to check the Agniveer Army Result 2026?**
You need your Roll Number (from your Admit Card) and your Date of Birth. No additional documents are needed to view the result online.

**My result shows "Not Qualified." Can I reapply?**
Yes. Candidates who do not qualify in one Agniveer recruitment cycle can apply in the next cycle, provided they still fall within the eligible age bracket (17.5 to 21 years) at the time of that recruitment.

**What is the Seva Nidhi amount for Agniveer Army 2026?**
The total Seva Nidhi corpus at the end of 4 years of Agniveer service is approximately Rs. 11.71 lakh, tax-free. This is made up of equal contributions from the Agniveer and the government, per the Ministry of Defence Agniveer scheme notification of June 2022.

**Is 25% confirmed for permanent enrollment from every batch?**
Yes. The Agniveer scheme specifies that 25% of each batch will be offered the opportunity to continue in regular Army service after 4 years, based on merit and organisational requirement, per the official scheme announcement.

**Where is the CEE conducted?**
The CEE is conducted at designated exam centres across India. The admit card issued before the CEE specifies your exam centre, date, and reporting time. The exam is not conducted at the rally ground.
